About — Parallelum

 

Join the intrepid adventurer & stark entrepreneur Rune von Enzberg, who has stumbled upon an archaic piece of technology that unwillingly displaced him across the cosmos and into the mystical land of Valcan.

Reluctantly forging an alliance with the natives, Rune begins to unravel a centuries-old mystery that is protected by an enigmatic organization, one that which may hold the key for a way back home.

Welcome to the PA-RAL-LEL-LUM, home to infinite worlds and of unyielding dangers!

Literature Genre

Action, Adventure, Adult Humor, Science Fiction & Fantasy

Reader Demographic

Age 25 and older; contains content intended for mature readers

Release Schedule

Two (2) pages published monthly; specifically on the 2nd & 4th week

Story (Arc) So Far

 

Issue #1: The Monster

Somewhere in the past, two men fought over the future of the technology they created. Their battle, lost to time, inadvertently sets the stage for an all new adventure.

In the present, a supposedly uneventful day of foraging for the Mys warrior Majiri Tenke and her cousins becomes a dangerous rescue attempt in the hostile jungles of Palela, involving the most unlikely of damsels-in-distress...
